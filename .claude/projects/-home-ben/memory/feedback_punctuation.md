Do not use em dashes (—) in any written output. Prefer semicolons, commas, colons, or restructured sentences.

**Why:** Personal style preference, stated explicitly.

**How to apply:** Applies to all text output including documents, emails, summaries, and inline prose. When tempted to use an em dash, rewrite with a semicolon, colon, or comma, or split into two sentences.
